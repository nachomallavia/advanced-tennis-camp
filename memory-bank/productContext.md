# Product Context: Advanced Tennis Camp

## Why this project exists
The Advanced Tennis Camp provides high-quality tennis training and recreational activities. The website serves as the primary digital presence to attract juniors and adults to their camps in Verbier, Switzerland.

## Problems it solves
- Provides a centralized platform for camp information.
- Facilitates bookings and inquiries.
- Displays the camp's facilities and atmosphere through visual content.

## User Experience Goals
- Fast-loading pages.
- Intuitive navigation across multiple languages.
- Engaging visual elements (animations, hover effects).
- Clear calls to action.
